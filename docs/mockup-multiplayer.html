Design notes

From scaffold (not redesigned)

Lobby multiplayer card · CreateGameSheet (invite friend) · CompletedGamesCard · SQBoardShell back button · realtime channel · push notifications.

Pill rules

Tappable. Active player gets ✨ + 2px purple border. Pill shows current total only — no "+X" delta badge.

Highlight colour

Opponent's last-played category gets pink-400 outline + tinted bg. Matches SQ tile-selection ring.

Completed games

Show last 10 (matches Rungles/Snibble/solo Yahdle). Not dismissible. Tap to open the comparison view (panel 4).
